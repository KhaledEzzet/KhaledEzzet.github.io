How AI Agents Can Automate Customer Support for SMEs

Practical guidance for small and mid-sized teams to roll out AI support agents safely.

Jul 18, 2024 · 6 min read · AQLAN Strategy

Why AI support is now viable

LLM quality and retrieval tooling make it possible to automate high-volume support without scripting every path.

Teams can start with FAQs, order lookups, and triage before expanding into deeper workflows.

What to automate first

Target intents that map cleanly to policies: returns, replacements, order status, and appointment changes.

Design clear escalation paths with conversation summaries for human agents.

Guardrails that matter

Ground every response in your knowledge base and transactional systems to avoid hallucinations.

Add rate limits, PII scrubbing, and QA review queues to keep quality high.
